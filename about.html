Our Story

Tianyuan and Alpha Vet Pharmaceutical was founded with a vision to revolutionize veterinary medicine through innovative pharmaceutical solutions. Our journey began with a small team of dedicated scientists and veterinary professionals who shared a common goal: to improve animal health and welfare through cutting-edge research and development.

Today, we have grown into a leading pharmaceutical company serving the veterinary industry worldwide. Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and ethical practices has earned us the trust of veterinary professionals and animal owners alike.
